## Position Rationale

**ServiceNow (NOW, 10%)** — The largest holding in the actual Claude
portfolio. Enterprise agentic-AI workflow platform with high-visibility
subscription revenue; Claude rotated *into* NOW in its most recent
publicized trades.

**Broadcom (AVGO, 9%)** — Claude's thesis: control of roughly 60–70% of the
custom AI accelerator (ASIC) market via hyperscaler relationships. One of the
portfolio's two 10% launch positions.

**Vistra (VST, 9%)** — The other 10% launch position. Independent power
producer levered to AI datacenter electricity demand; signed a ~$10B AI power
deal in June 2026.

**Eli Lilly (LLY, 9%)** — Portfolio's healthcare anchor; GLP-1 franchise
with a long runway. Diversifies the tech concentration.

**NVIDIA (NVDA, 8%)** — Added by the Claude agent ahead of the Iran
ceasefire; Blackwell/Vera Rubin ramp in full production per the June Computex
keynote. Momentum re-confirmed in late June.

**Reddit (RDDT, 8%)** — Claude's data-licensing thesis: recurring AI
training-data revenue from Google (~$60M/yr) and OpenAI on top of an
accelerating ads business.

**Microsoft (MSFT, 7%)** — Bought by Claude on April 7 (replacing Howmet
Aerospace). Claude has recently trimmed it, which is why it sits mid-book
here rather than top-3.

**Zeta Global (ZETA, 7%)** — AI-driven marketing cloud; one of Claude's
most recent adds.

**Magnite (MGNI, 5%)** — Sell-side programmatic ad platform; small-cap
torque on connected-TV ad growth.

**Pagaya (PGY, 5%)** — AI credit underwriting network; high-beta fintech.

**Ardelyx (ARDX, 5%)** — Biotech with commercial-stage products (IBSRELA,
XPHOZAH); one of Claude's recent additions.

**Inter & Co (INTR, 5%)** — Brazilian digital bank; Claude's first Latin
American position, bought after a ~14.5% post-earnings dip at ~8x forward
earnings with 25%+ loan growth.

**AngloGold Ashanti (AU, 5%)** — Gold-miner hedge held since launch; gold
has stayed strong (~$400+ GLD) through 2026's rate and geopolitical noise.

**Cash (8%)** — Dry powder for rebalancing and new positions, mirroring the
real portfolio's active-trading style.

## How to Use This

1. Fund a brokerage account (one that supports fractional shares is easiest).
2. Buy each position to its target percentage.
3. Rebalance when a position drifts more than ~3 percentage points from
   target, or when this file is updated — whichever comes first.
4. Alternatively, the genuine article: the Autopilot app
   (joinautopilot.com) lets you copy the real Claude portfolio
   automatically with live trade syncing.
