FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is my financial data safe?

Yes — in the strongest possible sense. LoanReady is a browser-side tool: your numbers are processed on your own computer and never transmitted, uploaded or stored anywhere. Close the tab and they're gone.

Do I need QuickBooks?

No. QuickBooks Online's "Profit and Loss by Month" export works out of the box, but a simple spreadsheet with Month, Revenue and Expenses columns from any accounting system (Xero, Wave, FreshBooks, or a plain spreadsheet) works too.

Will my lender accept this?

LoanReady organizes your own accounting data into the format lenders ask for — it isn't audited financials or a guarantee of approval. Loan officers routinely accept borrower-prepared packages like this; many prefer them to raw exports.

What if my numbers change during the application?

Re-paste and regenerate as many times as you like. Single-report buyers can re-print for 30 days; Unlimited subscribers, forever.

I'm a bookkeeper or loan broker — can I use this for clients?

That's exactly what the Unlimited plan is for. Generate a package for every client application and deliver it under your own engagement.
