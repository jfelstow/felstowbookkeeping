09Access & secure credential sharing
Please do not type passwords into this form. This form sends submissions via email, which is not safe for credentials. Here's how I handle access securely:
  • QuickBooks Online: Invite me as your Accountant User (no password needed). I'll send simple instructions.
  • Banks & credit cards: Most banks let you add me as a read-only user. Where that isn't possible, we'll handle credentials separately (see below).
  • Payroll & other tools: Almost all have an "add accountant / bookkeeper" option that doesn't require sharing your password.
  • When a password really is the only option: share it via Bitwarden Send (free, encrypted, self-destructing link) or by phone call. Never email it. How does this work? →
