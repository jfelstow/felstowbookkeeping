# Stage 3 — Firm notice (due + 14 days, standard)

Goal: leave with a specific payment date. Vague "soon" doesn't close this touch — the
reply gets logged as a promise-to-pay with a date, or the account advances.

Subject: Invoice {{invoice_number}} — {{days_past_due}} days past due, please advise payment date

Hi {{contact_first_name}},

Invoice {{invoice_number}} for {{amount}} is now {{days_past_due}} days past its
{{due_date}} due date, and we haven't heard from you about it.

Could you reply with the date we can expect payment? If cash timing is the issue, tell me —
we'd rather set up a workable date than chase, but we do need a firm commitment this week.

Pay online: {{pay_link}}

Thanks,

{{signature}}
