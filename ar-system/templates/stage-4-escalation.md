# Stage 4 — Escalation (due + 30 days, standard)

This stage is a PHONE CALL. The email below is the paper trail that accompanies it, sent
the same day. Attach a full statement of account.

## Call script notes
- Ask for the owner or whoever controls payments — not the person who's been ignoring emails.
- State facts, not feelings: invoice, amount, days past due, touches so far.
- Offer two doors: pay in full by a date, or a short payment plan starting now.
- Get a commitment with a date. Log it before hanging up.

## Follow-up email

Subject: Statement of account — invoice {{invoice_number}}, {{days_past_due}} days past due

Hi {{contact_first_name}},

Following up on our call today. As discussed, invoice {{invoice_number}} for {{amount}}
is {{days_past_due}} days past due; the full statement of your account is attached.

Confirming what we agreed: {{commitment_summary}}.

If that date passes without payment, we'll unfortunately need to place the account on
credit hold for future orders — which we'd genuinely rather not do.

Pay online: {{pay_link}}

{{signature}}
