How to export this from QuickBooks Online
  1. Go to Reports → Profit and Loss.
  2. Set the period to the last 12 months and Display columns by → Months.
  3. Click the export icon and choose Export to Excel or CSV, then open the file and copy everything, or upload it here.

A simple three-column format (Month, Revenue, Expenses) works too — from any accounting system or a spreadsheet.
