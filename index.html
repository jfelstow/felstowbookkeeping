Pricing

Ongoing bookkeeping is a flat monthly fee — no hourly billing, no surprise invoices. Most clients land in one of three tiers:

Essentials
$250–$450 /mo
Sole proprietors and simple setups — a few accounts and straightforward monthly books.
Most common
Standard
$450–$800 /mo
Established businesses with multiple accounts and light accounts payable / receivable.
Growth
$800–$1,500 /mo
Higher volume, payroll, job or class costing, or multiple entities.

One-time cleanup or catch-up work is quoted as a separate project, billed at $52/hour. Every engagement is scoped to your business — we’ll confirm exact pricing together after our call.
